About the Author

The author of this extension is Garrett Emrick. I am a senior at Miami University of Oxford, and this is my first extension I created, which I did all of the work at a hackathon.

I don't plan on updating it, unless people really enjoy it. Then I may improve upon it further.

How to Search

In order to do a search, there are a couple of things that you can do. You can either right click or highlight a name while browsing, such as a friends name on facebook, and you will then be prompted to make corrections/changes to the final name. You should remove nicknames and abbreviations for the best search.

You can also use this popup at any time to do a search using the search line above.
